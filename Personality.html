PERSONALITY

  • System oriented approach to learning
  • Highly selective when choosing a topic to engage in deeply

I have a natural attraction to systems with multiple moving parts with a drive to understand its underlying mechanisms and rules.I've realised that i'm either willing to deeply engage in a select few topics of concerns or only willing to go surface level

Positives

  • Being able to take self directed learning paths that leads to deeper intuitive understanding of a subject matter of interest
  • Excelling in courses that I find to be extremely interesting ( Object oriented Programming , Statistics, Discrete Math, Data Strcutures)

Challenges I face

  • Potential underperformance in subjects that don't trigger deep engagement
  • May appear inconsistent to educators ( highly engaged in one course, minimally engaged in others)
